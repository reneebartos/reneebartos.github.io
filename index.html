Enterprise Platform Migration & Systems Integration

ReportAll USA | Operations & Platform Strategy

Context & Goal:
The "Parlay 2.0" enterprise tier was managed via a legacy Excel-based system, requiring manual provisioning for every user addition or removal. The goal was to migrate 150+ corporate accounts to a new self-serve UI, eliminating operational bottlenecks and ensuring a scalable foundation for future growth.

The Process:
• Data Audit: Cross-referenced legacy spreadsheets with Salesforce records to verify admin contacts and active user counts.
• Strategic Outreach: Developed custom communication funnels tailored to account renewal dates and license types (Named vs. Concurrent).
• Technical Consulting: Facilitated virtual demos for enterprise admins to address "change fatigue," demonstrate the new UI, and confirm billing structures.
• Risk Management: Identified non-responsive "stragglers" and implemented a proactive follow-up cadence, eventually pausing invoices to prevent billing disputes and ensure service continuity.

The Solution:
Executed a zero-downtime, manual backend migration for every confirmed user seat. Transitioned the company from a "heavy-lifting" service model to a modern SaaS self-serve architecture, empowering clients to manage their own user permissions and payment methods.

The Outcome:
• Net-Positive Revenue Retention: Despite the forced migration, account value grew as clients identified needs for additional seats during the transition.
• Operational Scalability: Reduced manual admin overhead by nearly 100% for migrated accounts.
• Data Integrity: Synchronized all enterprise account data with the centralized platform, eliminating single points of failure.

Payment Gateway Optimization & Revenue Assurance

ReportAll USA | Operations & Billing

Context & Goal:
The company’s Stripe payment gateway had accumulated a backlog of failed transactions and duplicate subscription profiles across both single-user and corporate tiers. The goal was to audit the gateway to prevent double-billing, reduce chargeback risks, and recover stalled revenue.

The Process:
• Subscription Reconciliation: Conducted a line-by-line audit of the Stripe ecosystem to identify and isolate duplicate corporate and individual subscriptions.
• Failure Remediation: Investigated "failed" subscription statuses to determine the root cause (e.g., expired cards, system glitches) and cleared the backlog to reflect accurate active revenue.
• Proactive VIP Outreach: Established a semi-weekly auditing cadence to personally contact enterprise administrators regarding expiring payment methods, preventing service interruptions.
• Risk Mitigation: Cross-referenced active accounts against billing triggers to ensure no client was subjected to accidental double-billing during renewal cycles.

The Solution:
Executed a comprehensive cleanup of the payment infrastructure and established a recurring Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for failed payments. This created a frictionless, accurate billing environment that protected both the client experience and corporate revenue.

The Outcome:
• Involuntary Churn Prevention: Eliminated service interruptions for enterprise clients by proactively managing expired payment methods before accounts could be suspended.
• Customer Trust & Retention: Eliminated the risk of double-billing, preventing customer frustration and costly payment gateway chargeback fees.
• Revenue Clarity: Cleared out false "failed" metrics, providing leadership with a highly accurate dashboard of true recurring revenue.
